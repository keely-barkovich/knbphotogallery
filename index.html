Sports

This was an assignment for my SMAD 225 class. We were asked to capture three action shots and two feature shots of a JMU sports club practice or game/match. I chose to photograph JMU's Club Ultimate Team during practice.

Photo Story

For this 225 assignment, we were asked to create a photo essay or a photo story. I chose to do a photo story about a day in the life of a college student. I asked my friend Jess if I could follow her around for a day, and was able to capture the raw moments of everyday college life.

Lighting Setups

In this 225 assignment, we were able to capture 6 photos with various lighting setups. The setups include: Flash Freeze, Flash Reflector, Photo Illustration, Glass Illustration, Light Painting, Portrait with 3pt Lighting.

Composition

This was one of my first 225 assignments. Here we were asked to use a public space to explore the aspects of composition in photography: Rule of thirds, Lines, Symmetry, Space, & Composition.
